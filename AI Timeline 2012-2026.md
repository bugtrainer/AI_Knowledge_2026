# 📅 AI Timeline: 2012-2026

## Era 1: Research (2012-2020)
| Year | Event                                                                                                                   |
| ---- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2012 | AlexNet wins ImageNet → deep learning proven on images                                                                  |
| 2014 | [[GAN]]s invented by Ian Goodfellow                                                                                     |
| 2016 | AlphaGo defeats world Go champion ([[Demis Hassabis]], DeepMind)                                                        |
| 2017 | **"Attention Is All You Need"** paper → the [[Transformer]] is born ([[Aidan Gomez]] co-author)                         |
| 2018 | GPT-1 released (OpenAI). BERT released (Google). Turing Award to [[Geoffrey Hinton]], [[Yoshua Bengio]], [[Yann LeCun]] |
| 2019 | GPT-2 released — OpenAI initially withheld it due to "misuse concerns"                                                  |
| 2020 | GPT-3 released — demonstrated stunning few-shot capabilities                                                            |

## Era 2: Foundation Models (2020-2023)
| Year | Event |
|---|---|
| 2020 | GPT-3 shows that scaling works. [[Foundation Model]] era begins |
| 2021 | [[Anthropic]] founded by [[Dario Amodei]] & [[Daniela Amodei]] (ex-OpenAI). GitHub Copilot preview |
| 2022 | **ChatGPT launched (Nov 30)** - fastest-growing app in history. Stable Diffusion released. Midjourney launched |
| 2023 | GPT-4 released. Gemini announced. Llama open-sourced by Meta. [[Cursor]] launches. [[xAI]] founded |

## Era 3: Deployment & Agents (2024-Now)
| Year | Event |
|---|---|
| 2024 | Claude 3, Gemini 1.5, GPT-4o released. OpenAI o1 reasoning model. AlphaFold 3. [[Demis Hassabis]] wins Nobel Prize. [[MCP]] standard launched. Devin 1.0 announced |
| 2025 | [[OpenClaw]] goes viral (250K+ stars). World Labs launches Marble. [[Google Antigravity]] launches. [[Yann LeCun]] leaves Meta, raises $1.03B for AMI Labs. [[Manus AI]] acquired by Meta. Devin drops to $20/month |
| 2026 | GPT-5.4, Claude Opus 4.6, Gemini 3.1, Grok 4.20, Muse Spark released. [[NemoClaw (NVIDIA)]] at GTC. [[EU AI Act]] high-risk rules (Aug 2026). AI writes 30% of Microsoft's code |

---

> [!NOTE]
> The pace of AI advancement is accelerating. More happened in 2024-2026 than in the entire 2012-2020 era.
