# 🧠 Anthropic

## Overview
Founded by [[Dario Amodei]] and [[Daniela Amodei]] (both ex-[[OpenAI]]) with a safety-first mission. Created Claude — widely regarded as the most reliable and thoughtful AI assistant. Pioneered [[Constitutional AI]] alignment technique. Secured $7.3B+ in funding (2024), including $4B from Amazon.

## Flagship Models / Products
- **Claude Opus 4.6** — Most capable: 1M-token context, 14.5hr task window, deep reasoning
- **Claude Sonnet 4.6** — Balanced: fast, smart, default for most users
- **Claude Haiku 4.5** — Fastest: ultra-cheap for high-volume applications
- **[[Claude Code (Anthropic)]]** — Autonomous coding CLI agent
- **[[MCP]]** — Model Context Protocol, the "USB-C for AI tools"

## Key Facts (2026)
- **$18.4B+ valuation** — One of the most valuable AI startups globally
- Claude Opus 4.6 competing head-to-head with GPT-5.4 and Gemini 3.1
- Enterprise Claude adoption accelerating rapidly
- Constitutional AI approach enables alignment without massive RLHF data
- $4B investment from Amazon gives Anthropic access to AWS infrastructure

## Why Anthropic Matters
Anthropic represents the "safety-maximalist" approach to frontier AI: build the most capable models possible while investing heavily in alignment and interpretability research. Their bet is that safety and capability are complementary, not competing.
