# 🌊 DeepSeek

## Overview
Chinese AI lab that shocked the world by producing frontier-quality models at a **fraction** of the cost of US competitors. DeepSeek-V3 (671B MoE) and DeepSeek-R1 (open-source reasoning) proved that not only can open-source match closed-source, but that compute efficiency can be radically improved. Backed by quantitative hedge fund High-Flyer.

## Flagship Models
- **DeepSeek-V3** — 671B [[MoE]] model trained for <$6M (vs. $100M+ for GPT-4)
- **DeepSeek-R1** — Open-source [[Reasoning Model]] matching OpenAI's o1
- **DeepSeek-Coder** — Coding-optimised model
- **[[DeepSeek Chat]]** — Free consumer chatbot

## Key Facts (2026)
- V3 trained for **<$6M** vs. ~$100M for GPT-4 — the single most important AI efficiency breakthrough
- R1 proved pure [[Reinforcement Learning]] (without supervised fine-tuning) can develop sophisticated reasoning
- All models fully open-source on [[Hugging Face]]
- Triggered the "DeepSeek moment" in January 2025 — NVIDIA stock dropped 17% in a day
- Demonstrated that US compute advantages can be overcome through engineering
