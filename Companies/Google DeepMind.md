# 🔴 Google DeepMind

## Overview
Formed in 2023 by merging Google Brain and DeepMind under [[Demis Hassabis]]. The largest AI research organisation in the world. Created AlphaGo, AlphaFold (Nobel Prize 2024), and the Gemini model family. Has the most compute resources of any AI lab and the deepest bench of AI researchers.

## Flagship Models / Products
- **Gemini 3.1 Pro** — Flagship: 1M-token context, Deep Think mode, natively multimodal
- **Gemma 4** — Open-source small model family
- **[[Jules (Google)]]** — Asynchronous AI coding agent
- **[[NotebookLM]]** — AI research tool grounded in your documents
- **AlphaFold 3** — Protein structure prediction (drug discovery)
- **TPU v7 (Ironwood)** — Custom AI [[ASIC]] hardware
- **[[Google DeepMind Genie 3]]** — Interactive 3D world model
- **[[Google Search AI Mode]]** — Integrated AI search experience

## Key Facts (2026)
- [[Demis Hassabis]] won Nobel Prize in Chemistry (2024) for AlphaFold
- Gemini 3.1 Pro competing directly with GPT-5.4 and Claude Opus 4.6
- Google AI Mode rolling out in Google Search globally
- Gemma 4 open-source models widely adopted for edge/local use
- DeepMind's safety research team is one of the largest in the world
