# 🔵 Meta AI

## Overview
Meta (formerly Facebook) operates one of the world's largest AI research labs (FAIR). Made the strategic bet to **open-source** their frontier AI models (Llama series), fundamentally reshaping the AI landscape. Under [[Mark Zuckerberg]]'s leadership, Meta has invested $30B+ annually in AI infrastructure.

## Flagship Models / Products
- **Llama 4** — Latest open-source model family (small → large)
- **[[Meta AI (Muse Spark)]]** — First closed multimodal reasoning model with visual chain-of-thought
- **Meta AI Assistant** — Built into WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and Messenger
- **Llama 3.1 405B** — The most capable open-source model
- **SAM 2** — Segment Anything Model for vision

## Key Facts (2026)
- Llama open-source release spawned 10,000+ derivative models
- $30B+ annual AI infrastructure investment
- Acquired [[Manus AI]] for agentic capabilities
- [[Yann LeCun]] left to found AMI Labs; no longer Chief AI Scientist
- Meta AI assistant reaches billions of users via social apps

## Key People
