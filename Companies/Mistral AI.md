# 🇫🇷 Mistral AI

## Overview
Europe's most important AI company. Founded by [[Arthur Mensch]] and ex-DeepMind researchers in Paris. Champions the open-weight model approach, releasing competitive models under Apache 2.0. Represents European [[AI Sovereignty]]. Valued at $6B+ within 18 months of founding.

## Flagship Models
- **Mistral Large 3** — Flagship: open-weight [[MoE]], competes with GPT-4 class
- **Mixtral** — Mixture of Experts architecture model (8x7B)
- **Magistral 1.2** — Open-weight [[Reasoning Model]]
- **[[Devstral (Mistral)]]** — Agentic coding model
- **Ministral** — Edge-optimised small models
- **[[Mistral Le Chat]]** — Consumer chatbot

## Key Facts (2026)
- **$6B+ valuation** — One of the most valuable European startups ever
- Open-weight approach makes Mistral the go-to for enterprises wanting self-hosted AI
- Mixtral popularised [[MoE]] for smaller labs
- Strong EU government support as the champion of [[AI Sovereignty]]
