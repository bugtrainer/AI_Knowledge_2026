# 🟢 NVIDIA

## Overview
The infrastructure backbone of the entire AI industry. NVIDIA's [[GPU]]s (A100, H100, B200) and [[CUDA]] software platform train and run virtually all frontier AI models. Became the most valuable company on Earth in 2024, crossing $3T market cap. Founded by [[Jensen Huang]] in 1993 as a graphics card company.

## Flagship Products
- **Blackwell B200 GPU** — Latest AI training/inference chip
- **H100/H200** — Current workhorse GPUs at every major AI lab
- **[[CUDA]]** — Software ecosystem for GPU computing (4M+ developers)
- **NeMo** — Enterprise LLM framework
- **[[NemoClaw (NVIDIA)]]** — Robotics agent framework (GTC 2026)
- **[[NVIDIA Cosmos]]** — Training platform for world models and robotics
- **[[NVIDIA Vera CPU]]** — ARM-based CPU for AI workloads

## Key Facts (2026)
- **$3T+ market cap** — Briefly the most valuable company on Earth
- 90%+ market share for AI training chips
- Every frontier model (GPT, Claude, Gemini, Llama) is trained on NVIDIA GPUs
- US export controls restrict NVIDIA sales to China — turning GPUs into a geopolitical weapon
- GTC conference is the most important annual event in AI hardware
