# 🟢 OpenAI

## Overview
The company that triggered the AI era with ChatGPT in November 2022. Founded as a non-profit in 2015 by [[Sam Altman]], [[Elon Musk]], [[Ilya Sutskever]], and others. Transitioned to a capped-profit structure. The most widely-known AI brand globally with hundreds of millions of ChatGPT users.

## Flagship Models / Products
- **GPT-5.4** — Flagship: 1M-token context, computer use, adaptive thinking, multimodal
- **GPT-5.3-Codex** — Optimised for code generation
- **o3 / o3-pro / o4-mini** — [[Reasoning Model]]s for math, science, and complex logic
- **DALL-E 4** — Image generation
- **Sora 2** — Video generation
- **ChatGPT** — Consumer-facing AI assistant (fastest-growing app in history)

## Key Facts (2026)
- ChatGPT has hundreds of millions of monthly active users
- 1M-token context window with computer use capability
- Signed multi-billion-dollar compute partnership with [[Cerebras (WSE-3)|Cerebras]]
- CEO [[Sam Altman]] attended India AI Summit Feb 2026
- Introduced ads for free ChatGPT users in 2026 — sparking controversy
- $13B strategic investment from [[Microsoft]]
- Board crisis in Nov 2023: Altman fired then reinstated in 5 days
