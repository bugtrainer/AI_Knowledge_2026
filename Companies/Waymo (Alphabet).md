# 🚗 Waymo (Alphabet)

## Overview
The leading fully autonomous robotaxi service, operating commercially across 10 US cities.

## Flagship Models / Products
End-to-end neural network driving system

## Key Facts (2026)
- ~500,000 paid fully autonomous rides per week
- 3,000+ vehicle fleet
- Raised $16B at ~$126B valuation
- Expanding to 20+ more US cities plus London and Tokyo
- Zero human driver — fully autonomous Level 4
