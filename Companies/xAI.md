# 🚀 xAI

## Overview
[[Elon Musk]]'s AI company, founded in 2023 after his high-profile departure from [[OpenAI]]. Built the Grok model family and the massive "Colossus" GPU training cluster (100K H100s). Unique advantage: real-time access to X (Twitter) social media data for training and inference.

## Flagship Models
- **Grok 4.20 Beta 2** — Flagship: 2M-token context, multi-agent architecture, cross-verification
- **Grok 3** — Previous generation, highly capable
- **Aurora** — Image generation model

## Key Facts (2026)
- **Colossus Cluster** — One of the largest GPU clusters in the world (100K NVIDIA H100s)
- 2M-token context window — largest of any major model
- Multi-agent architecture uses multiple models debating for higher accuracy
- Real-time X (Twitter) data integration
- More permissive content policy than competitors
