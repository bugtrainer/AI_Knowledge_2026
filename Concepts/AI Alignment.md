## Definition
**AI Alignment** is the research field focused on ensuring AI systems pursue the goals that humans actually intend — and don't develop unintended or harmful behaviours as they become more powerful.

## Why It Matters in 2026
- As AI systems become more autonomous ([[AI Agent]]), alignment becomes critical
- [[Anthropic]] was founded specifically because [[Dario Amodei]] was concerned about alignment at OpenAI
- [[Ilya Sutskever]] left OpenAI to found Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI) to solve alignment
- The [[EU AI Act]] requires demonstrable alignment for high-risk AI systems

## Approaches
- [[RLHF]] — Learning from human preferences
- [[DPO]] — Direct preference optimization
- [[Constitutional AI]] — Self-critique against principles (Anthropic)
- [[Red-teaming]] — Adversarial testing
- [[Interpretability]] — Understanding model decisions
