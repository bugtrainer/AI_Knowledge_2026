# 🛡️ AI Safety

## Definition
**AI Safety** is the research field dedicated to ensuring AI systems are **safe and beneficial** — both today (preventing bias, hallucination, misuse) and long-term (preventing existential risk from superintelligent AI).

## Key Voices in AI Safety
- **[[Geoffrey Hinton]]** — The most prominent voice warning about existential AI risk
- **[[Yoshua Bengio]]** — Led the 2026 International AI Safety Report
- **[[Ilya Sutskever]]** — Founded SSI specifically for safe superintelligence
- **[[Dario Amodei]]** — Founded [[Anthropic]] as a safety-first lab
- **[[Timnit Gebru]]** — Focuses on present-day harms: bias and power concentration

## The 2026 International AI Safety Report
Led by [[Yoshua Bengio]], backed by **30+ countries**. The most comprehensive global assessment of AI risks.
