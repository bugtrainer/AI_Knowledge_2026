# 🏛️ AI Sovereignty

## Definition
**AI Sovereignty** refers to a nation's or organisation's strategic independence from specific AI providers — covering data, compute, and models. In 2026, 93% of executives factor AI dependency risk into strategy.

## Why It Matters in 2026
- European companies increasingly use [[Mistral AI]] to avoid US AI provider dependency
- China is building independent AI ecosystems ([[DeepSeek]])
- Geopolitical tensions make dependency on a single country's AI infrastructure risky
- The [[EU AI Act]] partially addresses data sovereignty concerns

## Key Relationships
- Champions: [[Mistral AI]], [[Arthur Mensch]]
- Regulation: [[EU AI Act]]
- Alternative: [[DeepSeek]] (Chinese AI sovereignty)
- Concern for: Enterprise strategy
