# ⚡ ASI

## Definition
**Artificial Superintelligence** — hypothetical AI that far surpasses human intelligence in every cognitive domain: science, creativity, social reasoning, strategy. Nick Bostrom's *Superintelligence* (2014) popularised fears of an unaligned ASI. [[Ilya Sutskever]] founded **Safe Superintelligence Inc.** in 2024 specifically to build ASI safely.

## How It Works (Conceptual)
1. Once [[AGI]] is achieved (human-level AI), recursive self-improvement could rapidly escalate to ASI
2. An ASI could design better AI architectures, generate its own training data, and optimise itself
3. The "intelligence explosion" hypothesis suggests the gap from AGI→ASI could be months, not decades
4. No consensus exists on whether ASI is achievable, beneficial, or controllable

## Why It Matters in 2026
- [[Ilya Sutskever]]'s $1B SSI company shows top researchers take ASI seriously enough to devote their careers to it
- [[AI Alignment]] is fundamentally about preventing ASI from pursuing goals misaligned with humanity
- The debate split the AI community: optimists ([[Dario Amodei]]) vs. doomers ([[Geoffrey Hinton]])
- ASI discourse directly shapes AI policy, including the [[EU AI Act]]

> [!tip] Key Fact
> Ilya Sutskever left OpenAI (the company he co-founded) in 2024 to start Safe Superintelligence Inc., saying: "This is the most important technical problem of our time." He raised $1B immediately.
