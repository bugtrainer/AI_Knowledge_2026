## Definition
**Direct Preference Optimization (DPO)** is a simpler, more efficient alternative to [[RLHF]] for aligning AI models with human preferences. Instead of training a separate reward model, DPO directly optimizes the language model using preference data.

## Why It Matters
- Simpler to implement than RLHF (no reward model needed)
- More stable training
- Increasingly adopted alongside or replacing RLHF in 2025-2026

## Key Relationships
- Alternative to: [[RLHF]]
- Part of: [[Fine-Tuning]]
- Goal: [[AI Alignment]]
