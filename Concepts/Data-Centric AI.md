## Definition
**Data-Centric AI** is a paradigm where data quality — not model architecture — is treated as the primary constraint on AI performance. Championed by [[Andrew Ng]], it focuses on systematically engineering the data used for training.

## Why It Matters
- "Garbage in, garbage out" — a better model trained on bad data will still produce bad results
- Responds to the risk of [[Model Collapse]] by curating high-quality human data
- Enterprise AI success often depends more on data quality than model selection
