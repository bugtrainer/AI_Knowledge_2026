## Definition
**Deep Learning** is a subset of machine learning that uses **multi-layered neural networks** (hence "deep") to learn representations from data. It is the foundational technique behind all modern AI — from image recognition to language models.

## The Three Godfathers
Deep learning was pioneered by three researchers who shared the 2018 Turing Award:
- **[[Geoffrey Hinton]]** — Backpropagation and deep neural networks
- **[[Yoshua Bengio]]** — Neural language models
- **[[Yann LeCun]]** — Convolutional neural networks ([[CNN]])

## Why It Matters in 2026
- Deep learning IS the engine of the AI revolution
- Every [[LLM]], every image generator, every voice model uses deep learning
- Without the foundational work of the 1980s-2010s, there would be no ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini
