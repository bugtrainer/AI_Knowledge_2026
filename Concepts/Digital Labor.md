# 👷 Digital Labor

## Definition
**Digital Labor** refers to AI behaving as a **worker-like entity** in structured business processes — completing tasks, making decisions, and collaborating with humans within established workflows. It's the operationalisation of [[AI Agent]]s within enterprise settings.

## Why It Matters in 2026
- Companies are deploying AI as "digital workers" alongside human employees
- [[Devin 2.0 (Cognition)]] acts as a full AI software engineer
- [[Jules (Google)]] works asynchronously on coding tasks (you can close your laptop)
- SAP, ServiceNow, and Salesforce are all embedding AI agents as "digital workers" in their platforms

- Enabled by: [[AI Agent]], [[Agentic AI]]
- Tools: [[Devin 2.0 (Cognition)]], [[Jules (Google)]], [[Manus AI]]
- Governance: [[EU AI Act]], [[AI Safety]]
