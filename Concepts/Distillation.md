# 🧪 Distillation

## Definition
**Knowledge Distillation** is a technique where a large, powerful "teacher" model is used to train a smaller, more efficient "student" model to mimic its behaviour — transferring knowledge while dramatically reducing size and cost.

## Real-World Analogy
A master chef (teacher) trains an apprentice (student) by demonstrating techniques. The apprentice won't know everything the master knows, but they'll learn the most important skills efficiently.

## Why It Matters in 2026
- How many [[SLM]]s achieve impressive quality despite small size
- Microsoft's Phi series uses distillation from GPT-4 outputs
- Critical for creating [[Edge AI]] models
- Combines with [[Quantisation]] for ultra-efficient deployment
