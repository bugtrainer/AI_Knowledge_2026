## Definition
**Edge AI** means running AI models **locally on devices** (phones, laptops, IoT hardware, robots) rather than sending data to cloud servers. Essential for privacy, speed, and offline use.

## Why It Matters in 2026
- **Privacy**: Your data never leaves your device
- **Speed**: No network latency — instant responses
- **Cost**: No API fees or cloud compute bills
- **Offline**: Works without internet connectivity
- **[[SLM]]s** + **[[Quantisation]]** + **[[NPU]]s** make powerful local AI practical

## Tools for Edge AI
- [[Ollama]] — Run models locally on Mac/PC
- [[AnythingLLM]] — Private RAG pipelines locally
- [[Jetson Nano]] — NVIDIA's edge AI computer
- [[ESP32]] — Microcontroller for IoT AI

## Key Relationships
- Models: [[SLM]], [[Quantisation]]
- Hardware: [[NPU]], [[Jetson Nano]], [[ESP32]]
- Tools: [[Ollama]], [[AnythingLLM]]
- Contrast: Cloud-based [[LLM]] inference
