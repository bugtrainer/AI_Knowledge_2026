## Definition
**Embodied AI** refers to AI systems that interact with the **physical world** through robotic bodies. The convergence of [[LLM]]s, computer vision, and [[World Model]]s is enabling humanoid robots and autonomous vehicles to operate in unpredictable real-world environments.

## Why It Matters in 2026
- **[[Figure AI]]**: Figure 03 operating autonomously in BMW factories using Helix AI
- **Tesla Optimus**: Gen 3 humanoid expected Summer 2026 with 22 degrees of freedom in hands
- **[[Waymo (Alphabet)]]**: ~500K paid autonomous rides/week across 10 US cities
- **Unitree & AgiBot**: Chinese humanoid robots scaling production rapidly
- Target: home-ready humanoid robots by end of 2026 (Figure AI)

## Key Relationships
- Requires: [[World Model]], [[LLM]], [[Multimodal AI]]
- Hardware: [[Jetson Nano]], [[NVIDIA Cosmos]], [[NPU]]
- Companies: [[Figure AI]], [[Waymo (Alphabet)]]
- End goal: [[AGI]]
