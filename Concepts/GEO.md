# 🌐 GEO

## Definition
**GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)** is the practice of optimising content to be **cited by AI models** — the new SEO. As people increasingly get answers from AI assistants (Perplexity, ChatGPT, Gemini) instead of Google Search, being cited by these AI models is becoming as important as ranking on Google.

## How It Differs from SEO
| Aspect | SEO | GEO |
|---|---|---|
| **Goal** | Rank on Google Search | Be cited by AI assistants |
| **Audience** | Search engine crawlers | AI models |
| **Format** | Keywords, backlinks | Cited facts, structured data |
| **Medium** | Search results pages | AI-generated answers |

## Why It Matters in 2026
- [[Perplexity AI]] processes 780M+ monthly queries with cited sources
- ChatGPT Search, Gemini AI Mode, and Grok all surface web content
- Businesses that aren't GEO-optimised risk becoming invisible to AI-powered discovery
