## Definition
**Grounding** means connecting AI outputs to verified, real-world information to reduce [[Hallucination]]. A grounded AI system provides answers traceable to specific sources.

## How It Works
- **[[RAG]]**: Retrieve from your own documents before generating
- **Web search**: Models like Perplexity search the web in real-time and cite sources
- **Citation requirements**: Force the model to reference specific sources
- **Structured data**: Connect to databases, APIs, or knowledge graphs

## Key Relationships
- Enables: [[RAG]], [[Prompt Engineering]]
- Reduces: [[Hallucination]]
- Tools: [[Perplexity AI]], [[NotebookLM]], [[AnythingLLM]]
