# 🔬 Interpretability

## Definition
**Interpretability** is the research field focused on understanding **why** an AI model makes specific decisions — opening the "black box" of neural networks. Also known as **Explainable AI (XAI)** or **Mechanistic Interpretability**.

## Why It Matters in 2026
- The [[EU AI Act]] **requires** explainability for high-risk AI systems
- Critical for trust in healthcare, finance, and legal AI applications
- [[Anthropic]] is a leader in mechanistic interpretability research — finding actual "circuits" inside models
- Key to detecting dangerous behaviours before deployment
