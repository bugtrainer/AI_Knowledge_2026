# 🔓 Jailbreak

## Definition
A **Jailbreak** is a prompt specifically designed to bypass an AI model's safety guidelines — tricking it into producing harmful, inappropriate, or restricted content that it would normally refuse.

## How It Differs from Prompt Injection
- **[[Prompt Injection]]**: Hijacking the model to follow *different* instructions (often for data theft)
- **Jailbreak**: Convincing the model to ignore its safety training (for harmful content generation)

## Why It Matters
- An ongoing cat-and-mouse game between model developers and adversaries
- Every frontier model gets jailbroken within hours of release
- Mitigated by: [[Guardrails]], [[Constitutional AI]], [[Red-teaming]]

## Key Relationships

- Tested by: [[Red-teaming]]
