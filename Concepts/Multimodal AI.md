## Definition
**Multimodal AI** refers to models that can process and generate across **multiple types of content**: text, images, audio, video, code, and structured data — all within a single system.

## Why It Matters in 2026
All frontier models in 2026 are multimodal:
- **GPT-5.4** — Text, images, audio, video, code, computer use
- **Claude Opus 4.6** — Text, images, code, documents, vision
- **Gemini 3.1 Pro** — Text, images, audio, video, code (native multimodal from the ground up)
- **Muse Spark (Meta)** — "Visual chain-of-thought" reasoning across modalities
