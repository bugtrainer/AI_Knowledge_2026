# Key People Overview

3. The Key People in AI in 2026

Understanding who shapes AI is as important as understanding the technology itself.

### 3.1 The Godfathers of Deep Learning

Three researchers laid the mathematical foundations for virtually all modern AI. They shared the 2018 Turing Award — computing's Nobel Prize — for their work on deep learning.

| Person | Role | Why They Matter | What They're Doing in 2026 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Geoffrey Hinton** | Professor Emeritus, Univ. of Toronto / formerly Google | Pioneered backpropagation and deep neural networks in the 1980s-2000s. Without his work there would be no ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. | Left Google in 2023 to speak freely about AI risks. Co-leads the 2026 International AI Safety Report. The most prominent voice warning about existential risks from advanced AI. |
| **Yoshua Bengio** | Professor / Institute Director, Univ. of Montréal / Mila | Co-pioneer of deep learning and neural language models. Founded Mila, one of the world's top AI research institutes. | Led the second International AI Safety Report (February 2026), backed by 30+ countries. Increasingly focused on AI governance and existential safety. |
| **Yann LeCun** | Founder & CEO, AMI Labs (formerly Meta Chief AI Scientist) | Pioneered convolutional neural networks (CNNs), the basis for all image recognition. Spent 12 years at Meta running FAIR. | Left Meta late 2025 after disagreements. Raised $1.03 billion for AMI Labs (March 2026) to build world models — a contrarian bet that LLMs alone will never reach AGI. |

| Person | Role | Why They Matter | What They're Doing in 2026 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sam Altman** | CEO, OpenAI | Leads the company that triggered the AI era with ChatGPT. Drives a push toward AGI as the primary goal. Publicly frustrated that AI adoption faces more resistance than expected. |

Regularly publishes essays on AI's economic impact. |

| **Jensen Huang** | Founder & CEO, NVIDIA | Founded NVIDIA in 1993. Made the GPU bet that became the backbone of all AI. Called OpenClaw 'the operating system for personal AI.' | Launched NemoClaw, Vera CPU, and Dynamo 1.0 at GTC 2026 (March). His annual GTC keynote is now one of the most important events in the AI calendar. |

| Person | Role | Why They Matter | What They're Doing in 2026 |
|---|---|---|---|
