# 🛡️ MOC — Safety & Alignment

> *Ensuring AI systems are safe, reliable, and aligned with human values.*

## Why Safety Matters
As AI systems become more capable, the potential for both benefit and harm increases exponentially. This MOC covers the technical, institutional, and regulatory efforts to keep AI safe.
