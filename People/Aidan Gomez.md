# 📄 Aidan Gomez
**CEO & Co-Founder, Cohere**

## Role & Significance
Co-authored the landmark **"Attention Is All You Need"** paper (2017) as an intern — the paper that introduced the [[Transformer]] architecture and changed everything. Founded [[Cohere]] to build enterprise-focused LLMs. One of the youngest and most influential figures in the AI industry.

## Key Contributions
- **"Attention Is All You Need"** (2017) — Co-authored the paper that invented the [[Transformer]]
- **[[Cohere]]** (2019) — Founded to build enterprise AI with focus on [[RAG]] and search
- **Enterprise AI** — Positioned Cohere as the enterprise alternative to consumer-focused ChatGPT/Claude

## In 2026
Leading Cohere's expansion in enterprise AI, focusing on retrieval-augmented generation and search applications.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "Every major AI advance in the last seven years traces back to that one paper." *(On the Transformer paper)*
