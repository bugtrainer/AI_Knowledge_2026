# 📊 Alexandr Wang
**CEO & Founder, Scale AI** — Youngest self-made billionaire in history

## Role & Significance
Founded Scale AI at age 19 to solve AI's data labelling problem. Scale AI provides the training data infrastructure used by nearly every frontier lab, including [[OpenAI]], [[Anthropic]], and the US Department of Defense. Became the youngest self-made billionaire in 2022. Champion of US AI competitiveness against China.

## Key Contributions
- **Scale AI** (2016) — Built the dominant data labelling platform for AI training
- **Government AI** — Major contracts with US DoD and intelligence agencies
- **Data-Centric AI** — Proved that training data quality is as important as model architecture
- **Policy Influence** — Active voice in US AI policy and national security discussions

## In 2026
Scale AI now provides evaluation benchmarks, data pipelines, and AI safety testing for government and enterprise clients.

> "Data is the new gold. In the AI race between nations, whoever has the best data wins."
