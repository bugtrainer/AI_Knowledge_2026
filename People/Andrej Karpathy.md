# 🎓 Andrej Karpathy
**AI Educator & Entrepreneur** — Founding member of OpenAI, former Tesla AI Director

## Role & Significance
One of the most influential AI educators alive. His "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" YouTube course is the best free deep learning education on the internet, taken by millions. Coined the term "[[Vibe Coding]]" (2025). Former founding member of [[OpenAI]] and Director of AI at Tesla (autopilot).

## Key Contributions
- **Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** — YouTube course that trained engineers who now work at every major AI lab
- **nanoGPT** — Minimal GPT implementation (~300 lines) widely used for learning
- **makemore** — Simple character-level language model for education
- **"Vibe Coding"** (2025) — Coined the term for building software by describing it to AI
- **Tesla Autopilot** — Led the AI team building Tesla's self-driving computer vision system

## In 2026
Active independent creator and educator. His social media posts on AI architecture and training insights reach millions. Considered the bridge between cutting-edge research and public understanding.

> "The hottest new programming language is English." *(On vibe coding and the future of software development)*
