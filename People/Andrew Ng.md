# 📚 Andrew Ng
**Founder, DeepLearning.AI** — Co-founder of Coursera, former Google Brain & Baidu AI leader

## Role & Significance
The world's most influential AI educator. Co-founded Google Brain and Coursera. Led Baidu AI Group. His Coursera Machine Learning course has been completed by **5M+ people** — the most enrolled ML course in history. Champion of [[Data-Centric AI]] and [[Agentic AI]] workflows.

## Key Contributions
- **Coursera ML Course** — 5M+ completions; the gateway into ML for an entire generation of engineers
- **Google Brain** (2011) — Co-founded Google's AI research division (now part of [[Google DeepMind]])
- **DeepLearning.AI** — The biggest online ML education platform
- **Data-Centric AI** — Championed the paradigm that data quality matters more than model architecture
- **Agentic Workflows** (2024) — His essay on [[Agentic AI]] defined 4 key agent design patterns adopted industry-wide

## In 2026
Continues leading AI education through DeepLearning.AI. Regularly publishes "The Batch" newsletter covering AI developments. His agentic workflow framework is now standard reference material at AI companies.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "AI is the new electricity. Just as electricity transformed every major industry 100 years ago, AI will transform every major industry in the next several years."
