# 🔍 Aravind Srinivas
**CEO & Co-Founder, Perplexity AI**

## Role & Significance
Built [[Perplexity AI]] into the leading AI-powered search engine with citations. Former OpenAI and Google researcher. At 30, he leads a company that directly threatens Google's search monopoly. Focuses on providing AI answers grounded in verified sources — solving the [[Hallucination]] problem through [[RAG]].

## Key Contributions
- **[[Perplexity AI]]** (2022) — Created the AI answer engine with citations
- **Citation-First AI** — Pioneered the approach of grounding AI answers in sources
- **Comet** — Perplexity's planned AI browser
- **Revenue Growth** — $100M+ ARR in 2024, one of the fastest-growing AI startups

## In 2026
Perplexity's Sonar API powering enterprise search. Launching [[Comet (Perplexity)]] browser. Competing directly with Google and ChatGPT for answer-engine dominance.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "The future of search is not a list of links. It's a direct, sourced answer."
