# 🇫🇷 Arthur Mensch
**CEO & Co-Founder, Mistral AI**

## Role & Significance
Former DeepMind researcher who co-founded [[Mistral AI]] in Paris — Europe's most important AI company. Champions the open-weight model approach, releasing competitive models under Apache 2.0 license. Built Mistral into a $6B+ company within 18 months. Represents Europe's push for [[AI Sovereignty]].

## Key Contributions
- **[[Mistral AI]]** (2023) — Founded Europe's frontier AI lab
- **Open-Weight Models** — Released high-quality models (Mistral, Mixtral, Large) as open-weight
- **MoE Leadership** — Mixtral popularised the [[MoE]] architecture for smaller labs
- **European AI** — Made Mistral a symbol of European AI independence and [[AI Sovereignty]]

## In 2026
Mistral Large 3 competing with global leaders. [[Devstral (Mistral)]] for agentic coding. Magistral for reasoning.
