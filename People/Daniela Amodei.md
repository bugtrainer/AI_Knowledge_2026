# 🏢 Daniela Amodei
**President & Co-Founder, Anthropic**

## Role & Significance
Co-founded [[Anthropic]] with her brother [[Dario Amodei]] in 2021. Previously VP of Safety & Policy at [[OpenAI]]. Handles the business, operations, and policy side of Anthropic while Dario leads research. One of the most powerful women in AI.

## Key Contributions
- **Anthropic** (2021) — Co-founded the leading AI safety company
- **Business Leadership** — Led Anthropic's fundraising ($7.3B+ in 2024 alone)
- **Policy & Safety** — Shaped Anthropic's responsible scaling framework
- **Amazon Partnership** — Negotiated the $4B investment from Amazon

## In 2026
Leading Anthropic's business operations through explosive growth. Managing enterprise Claude adoption and government partnerships.
