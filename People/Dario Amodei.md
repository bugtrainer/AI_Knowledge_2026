# 🧠 Dario Amodei
**CEO & Co-Founder, Anthropic**

## Role & Significance
Founded [[Anthropic]] in 2021 with his sister [[Daniela Amodei]] and other ex-OpenAI researchers. Former VP of Research at [[OpenAI]]. Strong advocate for [[AI Safety]] who believes frontier AI can be both incredibly powerful and responsibly developed. His 2024 essay "Machines of Loving Grace" is the most widely discussed AI optimism manifesto of the decade.

## Key Contributions
- **Anthropic** (2021) — Co-founded the safety-focused lab that created Claude
- **Constitutional AI** — Pioneered the [[Constitutional AI]] alignment technique
- **Scaling Research** — Co-authored key scaling papers at OpenAI before founding Anthropic
- **"Machines of Loving Grace"** (2024) — Essay arguing AI could accelerate biomedical breakthroughs by a decade in 5-10 years

## In 2026
Leads Anthropic through explosive growth. Claude Opus 4.6 competing head-to-head with GPT-5.4. Fundraising secured Anthropic at $18.4B+ valuation with $7.3B raised in 2024.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "I think the future is going to be really, really good... Much better than if AI had never existed. But that's only if we get safety right."
