# 🏆 Demis Hassabis
**CEO, Google DeepMind & Isomorphic Labs** — Nobel Prize in Chemistry (2024)

## Role & Significance
Founded DeepMind (2010), which became the most celebrated AI research lab in the world. Created AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and Gemini. Won the **2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry** for AlphaFold's protein structure predictions — the first AI system to win a Nobel. Chess prodigy turned AI visionary.

## Key Contributions
- **AlphaGo** (2016) — First AI to defeat a world Go champion, a feat thought decades away
- **AlphaFold** (2020) — Solved the 50-year-old protein folding problem; Nobel Prize 2024
- **Gemini** — Led development of Google's flagship AI model family
- **DeepMind** (2010) — Founded the lab acquired by Google for $500M in 2014
- **[[Isomorphic Labs (DeepMind)]]** — Using AI to accelerate drug discovery

## In 2026
Leads the unified Google DeepMind division. Gemini 3.1 Pro competing with GPT-5.4. AlphaFold 3 now predicts protein-ligand interactions, transforming drug discovery. Nominated for Time Person of the Year.
