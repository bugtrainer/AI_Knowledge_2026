# 🚀 Elon Musk
**CEO, Tesla, SpaceX & xAI** — Co-founder, OpenAI

## Role & Significance
Co-founded [[OpenAI]] (2015) then departed over disagreements with [[Sam Altman]]. Founded [[xAI]] (2023) with the stated goal of building AI that "understands the universe." Grok is xAI's chatbot. Criticized OpenAI's shift from non-profit to for-profit. Filed lawsuit against OpenAI in 2024.

## Key Contributions
- **[[OpenAI]] Co-Founder** (2015) — Provided early funding (~$100M) to start OpenAI as a non-profit
- **[[xAI]]** (2023) — Founded to compete with OpenAI, building Grok series
- **Grok 4.20** — xAI's flagship model with 2M token context and multi-agent reasoning
- **Colossus Cluster** — Built one of the largest GPU training clusters in the world (100K H100s)
- **Neuralink** — Brain-computer interface company at the intersection of AI and neuroscience

## In 2026
Leading xAI development of Grok models. Colossus training cluster expanded. Continues public feud with OpenAI. Active presence on X (Twitter) promoting xAI products.
