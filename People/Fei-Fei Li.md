# 📷 Fei-Fei Li
**Professor, Stanford University** — Creator of ImageNet, Co-Director of Stanford HAI

## Role & Significance
Created **ImageNet** (2009) — the dataset that proved deep learning worked and launched the computer vision revolution. Her conviction that "data is more important than algorithms" was vindicated when [[Geoffrey Hinton]]'s student built AlexNet on ImageNet data. Co-founded Stanford's Human-Centered AI Institute (HAI). Founded **World Labs** (2024) for spatial intelligence.

## Key Contributions
- **ImageNet** (2009) — 14M labeled images; the benchmark that sparked the deep learning revolution
- **Stanford HAI** — Co-directing the most influential academic AI policy institute
- **World Labs** (2024) — Founded to build Large World Models for 3D spatial intelligence
- **"Seeing is Understanding"** — Pioneered the idea that visual understanding is fundamental to intelligence

## In 2026
World Labs launched **Marble** — their first spatial intelligence product. Leading Stanford HAI policy work on responsible AI governance.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "There is nothing artificial about AI. It is made by humans, intended to behave like humans, and ultimately impacts humans."
