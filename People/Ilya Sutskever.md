# 🧬 Ilya Sutskever
**CEO, Safe Superintelligence Inc.** — Co-founder & former Chief Scientist, OpenAI

## Role & Significance
Co-founded [[OpenAI]] and served as its Chief Scientist, directing the technical vision through GPT-2, GPT-3, GPT-4, and o1. His 2012 AlexNet work (with [[Geoffrey Hinton]]) triggered the deep learning revolution. Led the Nov 2023 board attempt to remove [[Sam Altman]]. Founded **Safe Superintelligence Inc.** in 2024, raising $1B to build [[ASI]] safely.

## Key Contributions
- **AlexNet** (2012) — Co-created the CNN that won ImageNet and launched the deep learning era
- **GPT Series** — Directed the research that produced GPT-1 through GPT-4 at OpenAI
- **Scaling Philosophy** — One of the strongest proponents of "scale is all you need"
- **OpenAI Board Crisis** — Led the board's decision to fire Sam Altman (Nov 2023)
- **SSI** (2024) — Founded Safe Superintelligence Inc. with $1B funding

## In 2026
Leading SSI with singular focus on building aligned superintelligence. Rarely makes public appearances. His departure from OpenAI is widely seen as the most consequential personnel change in AI history.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "The next generation of AI will be so capable that the #1 priority becomes making sure it goes well for humanity."
