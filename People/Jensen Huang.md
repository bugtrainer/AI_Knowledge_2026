# 🟢 Jensen Huang
**CEO & Co-Founder, NVIDIA** — The man who bet the company on AI

## Role & Significance
Founded [[NVIDIA]] in 1993 as a graphics card company. Made the visionary bet that [[GPU]]s could power AI training, creating [[CUDA]] (2007) years before AI went mainstream. Now leads the company whose chips are the foundation of the entire AI industry. Under his leadership, NVIDIA became the most valuable company on Earth in 2024.

## Key Contributions
- **NVIDIA [[GPU]]s** — Made parallel computing accessible; A100/H100 are the "currency" of AI
- **[[CUDA]]** (2007) — Created the software platform that made GPUs programmable for AI
- **GTC Conference** — NVIDIA's annual AI keynote has become the most important hardware event in AI
- **AI Infrastructure Vision** — Correctly predicted "accelerated computing" would replace general-purpose CPUs

## In 2026
Launched Blackwell B200 GPU architecture. NVIDIA GTC 2026 revealed [[NemoClaw (NVIDIA)]] and next-gen inference chips. $3T+ market cap. Jensen's leather jacket and keynotes are iconic in the tech world.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "Software is eating the world, but AI is going to eat software." *(GTC 2024)*
