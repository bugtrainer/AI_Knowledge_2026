# 🎭 Joy Buolamwini
**Founder, Algorithmic Justice League** — MIT Media Lab Researcher

## Role & Significance
Computer scientist who exposed racial and gender bias in commercial facial recognition systems (Amazon, Microsoft, IBM). Her "Gender Shades" research demonstrating that AI identified lighter-skinned males with 99% accuracy but darker-skinned females with only 65% accuracy became seminal — leading to moratoriums on facial recognition by multiple police departments.

## Key Contributions
- **Gender Shades** (2018) — Research proving racial bias in facial recognition sold by major tech companies
- **Algorithmic Justice League** — Founded to fight AI bias and promote equitable technology
- **"Coded Bias" Documentary** (2020) — Netflix documentary featuring her work
- **Congressional Testimony** — Testified before US Congress on algorithmic accountability

## In 2026
Expanding Algorithmic Justice League's work to cover bias in LLMs and generative AI. Advocating for stronger provisions in the [[EU AI Act]].
