# 📖 Kate Crawford
**Senior Principal Researcher, Microsoft Research** — Author of "Atlas of AI"

## Role & Significance
Leading AI ethics researcher and author of *Atlas of AI* (2021), which revealed the hidden environmental, labour, and political costs of AI systems. Her work exposed how AI training exploits low-wage data workers, consumes massive energy, and encodes societal biases. Co-founded the AI Now Institute at NYU.

## Key Contributions
- **"Atlas of AI"** (2021) — Book exposing the material and social costs of AI
- **AI Now Institute** — Co-founded the leading AI policy research institute
- **Supply Chain Research** — Revealed lithium mining, energy costs, and labor exploitation behind AI
- **Microsoft Research** — Continues studying the societal impacts of AI systems

## In 2026
Publishing research on generative AI's environmental footprint. Advisory role on [[EU AI Act]] implementation.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "AI is neither artificial nor intelligent. It is made from natural resources, human labor, and existing systems of power."
