# 🔵 Mark Zuckerberg
**CEO, Meta** — Champion of open-source AI

## Role & Significance
Made the strategic decision to open-source Meta's Llama AI models — one of the most consequential choices in the AI industry. This decision commoditised foundation models and challenged the closed-source business models of [[OpenAI]] and [[Anthropic]]. Under his leadership, Meta AI released Llama 2, Llama 3, Llama 4, and invested billions in AI research.

## Key Contributions
- **Llama Open-Source** (2023-2026) — Released the world's most capable open-weight AI models
- **Meta AI Research** — One of the largest corporate AI research organisations
- **Reality Labs** — Pursuing AI + AR/VR convergence with Quest headsets
- **Muse Spark** — Meta's multimodal reasoning model (2026)

## In 2026
Meta AI continuing Llama development. Muse Spark released as Meta's first closed flagship reasoning model. Acquired [[Manus AI]]. Meta's annual AI compute spend now exceeds $30B.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "I think the right thing to do is to make AI as open as possible so that as many people as possible can benefit."
