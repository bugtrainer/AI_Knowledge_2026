# 🔬 Mira Murati
**Former CTO, OpenAI**

## Role & Significance
Led the engineering of ChatGPT, GPT-4, DALL-E, and Sora at [[OpenAI]]. Briefly served as interim CEO during the Nov 2023 board crisis. Departed OpenAI in Sept 2024 to found her own AI company. Albanian-born, she rose from Tesla engineer to one of the most powerful figures in AI.

## Key Contributions
- **ChatGPT Engineering Lead** — Oversaw the product launch that sparked the AI revolution
- **GPT-4 & Sora** — Led technical development of OpenAI's most advanced products
- **Interim CEO** (Nov 2023) — Briefly led OpenAI during the [[Sam Altman]] board crisis
- **Post-OpenAI Startup** (2024) — Founded new AI venture with undisclosed focus

## In 2026
Building her new AI company in stealth mode. Regarded as one of the most talented AI engineering leaders of the generation.
