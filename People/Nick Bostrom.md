# 📕 Nick Bostrom
**Philosopher, University of Oxford** — Author of *Superintelligence* (2014)

## Role & Significance
Author of *Superintelligence: Paths, Dangers, Strategies* (2014) — the book that put AI existential risk on the global agenda. Founded the Future of Humanity Institute at Oxford. His work directly influenced [[Elon Musk]], [[Ilya Sutskever]], and the entire [[AI Safety]] movement. Articulated the "paperclip maximiser" thought experiment.

## Key Contributions
- **"Superintelligence" (2014)** — The foundational book on [[ASI]] risks
- **Future of Humanity Institute** — Oxford research centre on existential risk
- **Paperclip Maximiser** — Famous thought experiment illustrating [[AI Alignment]] failure
- **Simulation Hypothesis** — Are we living in a computer simulation?

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "The biggest risk is not that AI will become malicious, but that it will become competent while pursuing goals that are subtly misaligned with ours."

## Key Connections
