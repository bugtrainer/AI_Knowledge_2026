# 🟦 Satya Nadella
**CEO, Microsoft**

## Role & Significance
Positioned Microsoft as the dominant platform for AI by investing $13B in [[OpenAI]] and embedding AI across all Microsoft products (Copilot, Azure, GitHub, Office 365). Under his leadership, Microsoft became the world's most valuable company, with AI as the growth engine.

## Key Contributions
- **$13B OpenAI Partnership** — Largest single AI investment in history
- **Microsoft Copilot** — AI embedded across Windows, Office, GitHub, and Azure
- **Azure AI Platform** — Made Azure the #1 cloud for enterprise AI deployment
- **"Every product will be AI-first"** — Reorganised Microsoft around AI-native products

## In 2026
Microsoft 365 Copilot achieving widespread enterprise adoption. Azure AI revenue growing 50%+ YoY. GitHub Copilot is the most used AI coding tool globally.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "AI is going to reshape every software category that exists."
