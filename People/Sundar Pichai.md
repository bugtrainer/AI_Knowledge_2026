# 🔴 Sundar Pichai
**CEO, Google & Alphabet**

## Role & Significance
Oversees the entire Google AI strategy, including [[Google DeepMind]] (merged Google Brain + DeepMind in 2023), Gemini, Search AI, and the Waymo autonomous driving division. Merged Google's rival AI teams into a unified force under [[Demis Hassabis]].

## Key Contributions
- **Google DeepMind Merger** (2023) — United Google Brain and DeepMind into a single powerhouse
- **Gemini Launch** — Oversaw development and launch of Google's flagship AI model family
- **AI-First Google** — Search AI Mode, AI Overviews, Gemini integration across Google products
- **TPU Investment** — Committed billions to Google's custom [[ASIC]] AI chips (TPU v7 Ironwood)

## In 2026
Gemini 3.1 Pro competing with GPT-5.4. Search AI Mode expanding globally. Google's AI revenue growing through Cloud AI and Workspace AI features.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "AI is the most profound technology we are working on today. More transformative than fire or electricity."
