# ⚖️ Timnit Gebru
**Founder, DAIR (Distributed AI Research Institute)**

## Role & Significance
Former co-lead of Google's ethical AI team, fired in 2020 after publishing a paper criticising large language models ("Stochastic Parrots"). Her dismissal became the highest-profile AI ethics controversy and galvanised the field of responsible AI. Founded DAIR to pursue independent AI ethics research. Vocal advocate for AI worker rights and bias mitigation.

## Key Contributions
- **"Stochastic Parrots" Paper** (2021) — Co-authored the influential paper questioning environmental costs and biases of LLMs
- **DAIR Institute** (2021) — Founded independent AI ethics research institute after Google dismissal
- **Gender Shades Research** — With [[Joy Buolamwini]], demonstrated racial and gender bias in facial recognition
- **AI Ethics Advocacy** — One of the leading global voices on AI fairness, bias, and worker rights

## In 2026
Continues leading DAIR's research on AI's impact on marginalised communities. Active voice in [[EU AI Act]] policy discussions.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "AI systems aren't neutral. They reflect the values, biases, and priorities of the people and data that built them."
