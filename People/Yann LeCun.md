# 🇫🇷 Yann LeCun
**Chief AI Scientist, Meta** — Turing Award 2018

## Role & Significance
Pioneered [[CNN]]s (LeNet, 1989), which became the foundation of computer vision. One of the three "Godfathers of Deep Learning" (with [[Geoffrey Hinton]] and [[Yoshua Bengio]]). The strongest public advocate for open-source AI and vocal critic of AI doom narratives. Champions [[World Model]]s as the path to true AI over [[LLM]]s.

## Key Contributions
- **Convolutional Neural Networks** (1989) — Invented CNNs / LeNet for digit recognition
- **Self-Supervised Learning** — Pioneered learning representations without labels
- **JEPA** — Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture, his bet on [[World Model]]s
- **Open-Source AI Advocacy** — Key voice behind Meta's decision to open-source [[Llama]]

## In 2026
Left Meta to found **AMI Labs** with $1.03B in funding — pursuing his vision for non-language-based AI (World Models / JEPA). Continues as the most active AI figure on social media, frequently debating [[AI Safety]] researchers.

> [!quote] Notable Quote
> "LLMs cannot be a path to AGI. They're just fancy autocomplete. Intelligence requires understanding the physical world — not just predicting the next word."

## Key Connections
