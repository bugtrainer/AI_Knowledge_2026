# The AI Landscape

2. The AI Landscape in 2026

2026 is the year AI moved from 'impressive demos' to real operational deployment. The big story is not new models — it is AI agents, AI infrastructure, and AI tools that work autonomously inside real workflows.

### 2.1 The Three Eras of AI

| Era | What It Is / Does |
|---|---|
| **Era 1: Research (2012–2020)** | Deep learning proved powerful. AI beat humans at chess, Go, image recognition. Mostly confined to labs. |
| **Era 2: Foundation Models (2020–2023)** | GPT-3, GPT-4, Claude, Gemini emerged. ChatGPT triggered global public awareness. |
| **Era 3: Deployment & Agents (2024–Now)** | The focus shifted from 'can AI do this?' to 'how do we deploy it reliably?' Agents, orchestration, governance, and reasoning models dominate 2026. Models now prioritise "thinking time" to solve complex logic before generating output (Reasoning Over Retrieval). |
