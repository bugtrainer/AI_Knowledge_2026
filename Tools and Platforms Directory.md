### 5.3 NotebookLM (Google)

NotebookLM is Google's AI-powered research and knowledge management tool — and the platform this very document was designed for. It is one of the most practically useful AI tools for knowledge workers in 2026.

**What Makes NotebookLM Unique:** Unlike general AI assistants that draw on broad training data, NotebookLM only knows what you tell it. You upload your own documents, and it becomes an expert on exactly those sources. This 'grounded' approach is far more reliable for professional and academic use than asking ChatGPT to summarise something it may hallucinate about.

**Key NotebookLM Features in 2026:**
- **Audio Overviews** — Converts your uploaded documents into a realistic, two-host podcast discussion.
- **Video Overviews** — Generates visual slide-style videos from your sources with AI narration and visuals.
- **Cinematic Video Overviews** — Immersive deep-dive videos with fluid animations.
- **Infographics** — Generates visual infographics from your sources in 10 styles.
- **Slide Decks** — Generates presentation slides from your documents, exportable as PPTX or PDF.
- **Flashcards and Quizzes** — Auto-generates study materials from uploaded sources, with saved progress.
- **Deep Research** — Multi-step research across sources producing comprehensive reports.
- **Gemini Notebooks Sync (April 2026)** — NotebookLM now syncs with the Gemini app.
- **NotebookLM Enterprise** — For businesses. Includes Model Armor security screening, audit logs, MDM deployment, and CrowdStrike security partnership.
- **Supported source types:** PDFs, Google Docs, Google Slides, websites, YouTube videos, audio files, EPUB ebooks, text files. Powered by Gemini 3 models as of March 2026.
