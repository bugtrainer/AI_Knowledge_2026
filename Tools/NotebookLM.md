# 📓 NotebookLM

## Definition
**NotebookLM** is Google's AI-powered research and knowledge management tool. Unlike general AI assistants, NotebookLM **only knows what you tell it** — you upload your own documents, and it becomes an expert on exactly those sources. This "grounded" approach is far more reliable for professional use.

## Key Features in 2026
- **Audio Overviews** — Converts documents into a realistic two-host podcast
- **Video Overviews** — Generates visual videos from your sources
- **Cinematic Video Overviews** — Immersive deep-dive videos with animations
- **Infographics** — Generates visual infographics in 10 styles
- **Slide Decks** — PPTX/PDF export from your documents
- **Flashcards & Quizzes** — Auto-generated study materials with progress tracking
- **Deep Research** — Multi-step research across sources
- **Gemini Sync** — Syncs with the Gemini app (April 2026)
- **Enterprise Edition** — Model Armor, audit logs, CrowdStrike security
