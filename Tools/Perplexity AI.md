# 🔍 Perplexity AI

## What It Does
AI-powered answer engine that provides direct answers with **inline citations** from real sources. Combines [[RAG]] with web search to give you researched, sourced answers instead of a list of links. Direct competitor to Google Search.

## Key Features
- **Cited Answers** — Every claim is sourced with clickable references
- **Pro Search** — Multi-step research with clarifying questions
- **Sonar API** — Enterprise-grade AI search API for developers
- **Collections** — Save and organise research into shared collections
- **Focus Modes** — Academic, Writing, Math, and Code-specific search

## Who It's For
Researchers, students, journalists, and anyone who needs factual, sourced answers instead of SEO-optimised blog posts.
